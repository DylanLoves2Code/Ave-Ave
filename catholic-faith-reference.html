Sacred Relics

The venerable remains and objects associated with the saints and Christ

The Three Classes of Relics

First Class

The physical remains of a saint: bones, hair, flesh, or ashes. Also includes instruments of Christ's Passion (the True Cross, the Holy Nails). These are the most venerated class of relics.

Second Class

Items owned or used by a saint during their lifetime, such as clothing, tools, books, or rosaries. These carry the sanctifying influence of the saint's contact.

Third Class

Objects touched to a first- or second-class relic. These are the most common type of relic distributed to the faithful and are used for personal devotion.
